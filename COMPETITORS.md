# PostPilot Pro — Competitive Analysis

---

## The Gap

Every major social media tool is built for marketing teams at medium-to-large businesses.
None of them are built for a solo food truck owner at 6am who needs to post their location before the lunch rush.

The gap:
- **Too expensive** (Hootsuite $99+) OR **too limited** (Meta Suite free, FB/IG only)
- **No Google Business posting** in any affordable tool
- **No website integration** in any tool at any price
- **No content generation** — you still have to know what to write
- **No food/hospitality-specific workflows** — location posts, daily specials, hours

## Moat — Why Users Won't Leave

Once a user is fully set up, switching away requires:
1. Reconnecting 6 platforms to a new tool
2. Removing the embed script from their website
3. Losing post history and analytics
4. Losing AI-learned business voice and templates
5. Finding a tool that does Google Business (none do at this price)
6. Finding a tool with website integration (none do at any price)

This mirrors the stickiness model of Mailchimp and HubSpot — high setup cost = near-zero churn.
