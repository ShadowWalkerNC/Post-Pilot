## Channel 2 — POS Marketplace Integrations

### Square App Marketplace (Priority #1)
- **120,000+ restaurant/food truck sellers**
- Free to list
- Integration: webhook fires when new menu item added → auto-post prompt
- Revenue share: Square takes ~15% of upgrades through marketplace
- **This is the fastest path to distribution**

### Toast Partner Marketplace
- **120,000+ restaurant customers**
- Requires formal partner application
- Same webhook model as Square
- Slower to get listed but high-value customers

### Clover App Market
- Retail + food service crossover
- Good for cafes and quick-serve restaurants

### Integration Pattern (Same for All POS)
```
POS Event: New menu item added
    ↓
Webhook POST to PostPilot Pro API
    ↓
Dashboard notification: "New item detected — post it?"
    ↓
User approves with one tap
    ↓
Published to all platforms
```

## Channel 3 — Agency White-Label

### Economics
```
You charge agency:      $249/mo (25 client slots)
Agency charges client:  $79/mo × 25 = $1,975/mo
Agency margin:          $1,726/mo
Your revenue:           $249/mo guaranteed
```

### What Agencies Get
- Their logo / brand on the dashboard
- Client management portal (add/remove clients)
- Bulk posting across all clients
- Reseller pricing (no PostPilot Pro branding visible to clients)
- Priority support

## Channel 5 — Passive / Organic

- **GitHub open source** → developers find it, star it, write about it, drive non-technical customers to SaaS
- **"Powered by PostPilot Pro"** footer on every hosted website → seen by every customer of every user
- **Food truck Facebook/Reddit groups** → direct community where primary customers already hang out
- **Product Hunt** → one launch, sustained long-tail discovery traffic
- **SEO** → "post to all social media at once food truck" — low competition, high intent keywords
- **Affiliate program** → food truck bloggers and influencers earn 20% recurring commission
