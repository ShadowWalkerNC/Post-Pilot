Website Hub — how it works

Your site. Your content.
Kept in sync, automatically.

Post-Pilot doesn’t build your website — it connects to the one you already have. Here’s exactly how that works.

1

Copy your embed snippet

In Settings → Website Hub, Post-Pilot generates a unique <script> tag tied to your account. One line of code, no framework required.

2

Paste it into your site’s <head> or use a webhook

Works with WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify, custom HTML — any site that lets you add a script tag. Prefer server-side? Use the webhook URL instead and receive a POST payload on every new post.

3

Post-Pilot detects the connection

Once the script is live, Post-Pilot pings your domain to verify the connection. Your site shows as Connected ✓ in your dashboard within seconds.

4

Content pushes live automatically

Every time a post is scheduled, Post-Pilot also sends the content to your site’s feed widget — announcements, daily specials, location updates — all without you having to log into your website.

Embed snippet One-time setup 
<!-- Paste inside your <head> -->
<script
  src="https://cdn.postpilot.io/hub.js"
  data-token="pp_live_abc123xyz"
  data-theme="dark"
  async
></script>

<!-- Drop the feed widget anywhere on your page -->
<div id="postpilot-feed"></div>
